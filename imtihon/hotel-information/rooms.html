Rooms

All our rooms were renovated in 2017 with new beds, soft cotton sheets, rainfall showers and complimentary high speed internet.  Enjoy the unique Amsterdam experience of waking up and looking out over a tranquil canal or enjoy people watching in lively Waarmoestraat from your room.  The hotel is housed in three historic buildings opposite each other, our room sizes do vary and are accessible by stairs. 

Please let us know at the time of booking if you have any special room requests and kindly note all requests are subject to availability on the day of arrival and cannot be guaranteed in advance.
