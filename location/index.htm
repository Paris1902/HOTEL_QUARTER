Location

Enjoy the perfect location to explore Amsterdam's top attractions and famous nightlife all within easy walking distance of your hotel.  Located on Warmoesstraat one of the most popular streets in the Red Light area and very lively at weekends, Old Quarter guests will find themselves spoilt for choice due to the large number of bars, restaurants and cafes on their doorstep.

Distance from local attractions and landmarks

  • 50m Red Light District

  • 450m Central Railway Station

  • 500m Dam Square

  • 2.4kms Heineken Experience

Directions from Amsterdam Schiphol Airport:
Take the train to Amsterdam Centraal Station approx 15-20 minutes.

Directions from Amsterdam Centraal Station
Exit Centraal Station and head south east towards Prins Hendrikkade and turn left.
Take a slight right and continue onto Nieuwebrugsteeg.
Turn right onto Waarmoestraat.
Old Quarter Hotel, your Amsterdam home is on the right.

Parking and Valet Parking
We are pleased to arrange guest valet parking 1-2 hours prior to arrival only, kindly note car parking is not available at the hotel.
